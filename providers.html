TECHNOLOGY

Neovare is the only test available in the market for individuals who:

• Meet or do not meet NCCN guidelines, and/or

• Have or do not have insurance coverage

1. NEOVARE DIAGNOSTIC: This test is for patients who meet NCCN guidelines. Since they are at high risk, we combine two different technologies when applicable (NGS and Microarray). This dual platform is the most comprehensive methodology available to date.

2. NEOVARE SCREEN: If you do not meet the NCCN guidelines yet still wish to get tested, it would not be for any medical necessity but for your own peace of mind. This does not mean that you are at zero risk of hereditary cancer, but that your risk is not higher than the general population. If you still wish to be tested, we offer you the Microarray platform at an out of pocket price of $150. This test detects all 24,000 known significant hereditary cancer mutations.

To determine which test you are eligible for, please fill the 3 minute NEOVARE DNAwareTM questionnaire at your physician’s office or online at www.neovare.com

The NEOVARE Guarantee:To ensure appropriate testing and avoid insurance denials, the Neovare pathologists and billing team will determine the indicated tests in light of the NCCN guidelines and your individual insurance policy. If necessary, we will contact you prior to testing to avoid any surprise bills.

NEXT GENERATION SEQUENCING: Neovare is utilizing massive parallel next generation sequencing technologies (NGS) to open the horizons for personalized medicine and provide precise approach to disease prediction, prevention and medical management. Our team of board certified pathologists, molecular scientists and genetic counselors are bringing the state of the art technologies into practice to improve patient care .
